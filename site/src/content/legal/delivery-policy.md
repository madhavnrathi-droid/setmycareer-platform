# Shipping & Delivery / Service-Delivery Policy

SetMyCareer is a **fully digital** service. There is **no physical shipping** and nothing is posted or couriered to you. This page explains how and when you receive access to what you buy, and how to get help if something has not arrived.

## 1. No physical goods — digital delivery only

All SetMyCareer products and services are **digital** and are delivered **electronically** to your registered account, email or phone. This includes psychometric assessments, the AI career coach (text and voice), live video/voice counselling and expert sessions, and AI-generated career reports.

Because nothing physical is shipped:

- there are **no shipping charges, no delivery addresses and no courier tracking**; and
- you need a **compatible device and a working internet connection** to access the service (a modern web browser; for live sessions, a working camera/microphone as applicable).

## 3. If delivery is delayed or something is missing

If you have paid but cannot see or access what you bought:

1. **Refresh and re-log in** to your SetMyCareer account, and check the email/phone registered on the account (including spam/junk).
2. **Check payment status** — occasionally a payment is still being confirmed by the bank or gateway; access appears once it clears.
3. **Contact support** (Section 4) with your order/payment reference. We will restore or re-deliver access, or, where we cannot, follow our **Refund & Cancellation Policy**.

If a delay or non-delivery is caused by a **technical failure at our end**, we will make it right — by re-delivering access, rescheduling a session at no cost, or refunding it. Delays caused by factors **outside our control** (for example your device, connectivity, or a third-party payment provider) are not delivery failures on our part, but we will still help you get access.
