## 1. Scope and who this applies to

This Policy applies to everyone who buys or uses a paid SetMyCareer product or service, including:

- individual clients (students, including minors purchasing through a parent or guardian, and working professionals);
- schools, colleges, employers and other organisations buying in bulk or under a B2B agreement; and
- users in **India**, the **United States** and elsewhere.

Where the rules differ by country, we say so in clearly-labelled subsections. Where a payment is processed for us by **Razorpay** (India) or a card processor (for US and international cards), refunds are always returned through that same processor to your original payment method.

## 3. Free items

Free trials, free or sample assessments, free chat with the AI career coach where offered, blog content, newsletters and other no-cost items involve no payment and are therefore not subject to any refund. You can simply stop using them at any time.

---

## 4. Paid psychometric assessments

Our assessments (Big Five personality, RIASEC interests, aptitude and similar) are **digital guidance instruments** delivered instantly on purchase. They are **guidance tools, not clinical, medical or diagnostic tests.**

- **Before you start:** if you have bought an assessment but have **not yet begun** it (you have not opened or started answering it), you may cancel and receive a **full refund**.
- **Once you start or complete an assessment:** because the assessment content and scoring are delivered and consumed the moment you begin, it is **non-refundable** once started, save where the law gives you a mandatory right (see Sections 9 and 11) or where the assessment or its results are **defective, materially inaccurate through our fault, or inaccessible because of a technical fault at our end** — in which case we will re-run it, fix it, or refund it.
- **Technical failure during an assessment:** if a fault in our system prevents you from completing an assessment you paid for, contact us and we will restore your access to complete it, or refund it if we cannot.

## 5. AI career coach (text chat and voice agent)

Where the AI career coach — including the live **voice agent** — is sold as part of a paid plan, access is delivered immediately and is **consumed as you use it**. Usage-based or already-accessed AI coaching is **non-refundable**, except where a fault at our end made the feature substantially unavailable for the period you paid for, in which case we will extend your access or refund the affected portion.

The AI coach provides **automated, AI-generated guidance**. It is not a human counsellor, not professional advice, and **not a mental-health, crisis, emergency or medical service.** Dissatisfaction with AI-generated guidance, on its own, is not a defect and is not a ground for refund; but a technical failure that stops the service from working is.

## 6. Live counselling and expert sessions

This Section covers live video/voice sessions with our certified human **counsellors ("navigators")** and with independent **domain experts** on our marketplace (typically 45-minute sessions). Sessions are **recorded and transcribed**, and you keep your report, transcript and video (see our Terms and Privacy Policy for how recordings are handled).

### 6.1 Rescheduling and cancelling by you

- **24 hours or more before the scheduled start:** you may reschedule or cancel free of charge. If you cancel (rather than reschedule), you receive a **full refund** or a credit of equal value, your choice.
- **Less than 24 hours before the start (late change):** the session is treated as booked and delivered. **No refund** is due. As a goodwill gesture we may, at our discretion and usually **once**, offer to reschedule you to a later slot.
- **No-show:** if you do not join a scheduled session and did not cancel or reschedule in time, the session is treated as **delivered** and is **non-refundable**. Please allow for a reasonable grace period — if your counsellor or expert fails to appear, that is covered by Section 6.3, not this rule.

### 6.2 Sessions completed or partly delivered

- A **completed** session is non-refundable.
- If a session is **cut short by a fault at our end** (for example our platform drops the call), we will reschedule the remaining time at no cost or refund a fair proportion.
- If a session is cut short by a problem at **your** end (for example your own internet or device), we will try to help you rejoin, but the session is treated as delivered.

### 6.3 When we cancel (counsellor/expert unavailability or technical failure)

If **we** cancel a confirmed session — because a counsellor or expert is unavailable, or because of a technical failure on our side — you may choose either:

1. a **free reschedule** to the next mutually convenient slot; or
2. a **full refund** of that session (or restoration of the session credit to your package).

We will tell you as soon as we can and will not charge you any rescheduling fee in these cases.

## 7. AI-generated career reports and other custom deliverables

Career reports are **individually generated** for you from your inputs and assessment results and are therefore **custom digital deliverables**.

- **Before generation:** if you have ordered a report but generation has **not yet begun**, you may cancel for a **full refund**.
- **Once generation has started or the report has been delivered:** the report is **non-refundable**, because it is prepared specifically for you.
- **If a delivered report is defective** — for example it is corrupted, materially incomplete, or does not match the assessment you took, in each case through our fault — we will **regenerate or correct it** at no charge, or refund it if we cannot put it right.

**Important — reports are guidance, not a promise of outcomes.** Our reports, assessments and counselling provide **career guidance only**. They are **not a guarantee** of any admission, examination result, job, internship, salary, promotion or other outcome. Not achieving a hoped-for outcome is not a defect in the service and is not, by itself, a ground for a refund.

## 8. Packages, bundles, credits and validity

### 8.1 How packages and credits work

Some products are sold as **packages or bundles** (for example, a set of sessions plus assessments plus a report) or as **credits** you redeem for individual services, often at a discount to buying each item separately.

- **Wholly unused package, cancelled within 7 days:** if you have **not used any part** of a package or credit bundle and you cancel **within 7 days** of purchase, you may cancel for a **full refund**.
- **Partly used package:** if you have used part of a package, you may cancel the remainder. We will refund the value of the **clearly unused, separable items only**, calculated at their standalone price, **less the bundle discount** you received (so we recover the discount that was conditional on buying the whole bundle). The parts you have already used or accessed are not refundable.
- **Credits** are treated the same way: unused credits within validity may be refunded on the basis above; redeemed credits are not.

### 8.2 Validity and expiry

Unless a longer period is stated on the product page or your order confirmation:

- **counselling sessions and session credits** must be scheduled and used within **30 days** [CONFIRM standard validity] of purchase; and
- **assessments and reports** included in a package should be taken/requested within the same validity window.

We will make reasonable efforts to remind you before credits expire. **Expired, unused credits are not refundable**, except where a mandatory law provides otherwise (see Section 11) or where you could not use them because of a fault at our end, in which case we will extend the validity.

## 9. Cooling-off and statutory cancellation rights

Nothing in this Policy limits any **non-waivable statutory right** you may have.

### 9.1 India (Consumer Protection Act, 2019 & E-Commerce Rules, 2020)

We do not use unfair trade practices, and we do not refuse to take back deficient services or refund amounts paid for services that are deficient, within a reasonable time. If a paid service is **deficient, not as described, or not delivered**, you are entitled to have it made good or refunded. Digital services that have been **fully delivered and consumed with your consent** (for example, a completed assessment or a delivered custom report) are not returnable simply because you changed your mind, but your rights in respect of **deficient** services are unaffected.

### 9.2 United States

US consumer-protection law is largely at the state level and does not generally impose a blanket "cooling-off" right for online digital services. The refund rights in this Policy apply to US customers. Where a specific state law, or the terms of your card scheme, gives you additional rights, those rights are unaffected. See also the chargeback note in Section 12.

Please include: your **name and registered email/phone**, the **order or payment reference**, the **item(s)** concerned, the **date of purchase**, and a **short reason** (and, for defects, any screenshots or details that help us investigate).

### 11.2 Request window

Unless a mandatory law or a specific product term gives you longer, please raise a refund request:

- for a **defective or non-delivered** service, within **7 days** of the problem arising; and
- for **cancellation of an unused package/credit**, within the window in Sections 8 and 9.

Requests outside these windows may still be considered at our discretion, especially where a fault at our end is involved.

### 11.3 Our decision timeline

- We will **acknowledge** your request within **24–48 hours**.
- We will **review and decide** it within **5–7 business days**, and tell you the outcome and reason. If we need more information from you, that period pauses until you reply.

### 11.4 Refund processing and how long it takes

- Approved refunds are always made to your **original payment method** through the original processor (**Razorpay** for Indian payments; the relevant **card processor** for US/international cards). We do not refund to a different account or by cash.
- We will **initiate** the refund within **2–3 business days** of approval.
- After we initiate it, the amount typically reaches you within **5–10 business days**, depending on your **bank, card issuer or payment provider**. UPI and wallet refunds are often quicker; international card refunds can take longer. These downstream timelines are controlled by your provider, not by us.
- Refunds are made in the **original currency** of the transaction. Any currency-conversion differences, or bank/gateway charges deducted by third parties, are outside our control.

If you have not received an approved refund within the expected time, contact us and we will share the refund/transaction reference so you can follow it up with your bank.

## 12. United States — chargebacks and card disputes

If you are a US (or other card) customer and you believe a charge is wrong, **please contact us first** at info@setmycareer.com — we can usually resolve it faster than a formal dispute. You retain the right to raise a **chargeback** with your card issuer under the applicable card-network rules. If you file a chargeback, we may share relevant records of your purchase and use of the service (booking, delivery and access logs, and this Policy) with the processor and your issuer to respond to the dispute. Filing a chargeback for a service that was properly delivered, instead of using the refund process above, may lead us to suspend the related account pending resolution.
