## 4. Scope of services & professional standards

### 4.1 What you will do
You will deliver career **counselling, guidance and advisory** Services — which may include live video/voice sessions, assessment debriefs, contributing to or reviewing AI-generated career reports, and follow-up notes — competently, in good faith, and in the Client's best interests.

### 4.2 Code of conduct
While providing Services you will:
- act with honesty, professionalism, cultural sensitivity and respect for every Client;
- stay within your area of competence and the scope of your engagement, and refer or escalate when a matter is beyond it;
- give **guidance, not guarantees** — you must not promise any Client a specific admission, job, salary, visa, examination result or other outcome, and you must not state or imply that SetMyCareer guarantees such outcomes;
- keep sessions free of discrimination, harassment, bullying or any sexual, exploitative or otherwise inappropriate conduct;
- follow our safeguarding and Client-safety policies, including our rules for working with minors and for handling wellbeing concerns (Section 4.4); and
- comply with all applicable laws, including consumer-protection, advertising, anti-discrimination and data-protection laws in India and, where relevant, the United States.

### 4.3 Not a medical, clinical or crisis service
SetMyCareer provides **career guidance**. It is **not** a mental-health treatment, counselling-for-illness, diagnostic, crisis or emergency service. You must not represent the Services as clinical or medical care, must not diagnose or treat any condition, and must not use the psychometric instruments on the Platform (Big Five, RIASEC, aptitude) as clinical or diagnostic tests — they are validated **guidance** instruments only.

### 4.4 Wellbeing and crisis situations
If wellbeing signals surface in a session, respond with care and within your competence, and follow our escalation policy. Where a Client appears to be at risk of harm, do **not** attempt to provide crisis treatment through the Platform; direct them to appropriate emergency and crisis resources and escalate to SetMyCareer as our policy requires.

- **India:** Tele-MANAS **14416** / **1-800-891-4416**; KIRAN **1800-599-0019**; Vandrevala Foundation **+91-9999666555**; iCall **+91-9152987821**; emergency **112**.
- **United States:** **988** Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988); Crisis Text Line (text **HOME** to **741741**); emergency **911**.

### 4.5 Availability and delivery
You will honour the sessions you accept, keep to scheduled times, give reasonable notice if you must reschedule, and use the Platform's tools for booking, delivery, recording and record-keeping. Persistent no-shows, late cancellations or off-platform delivery may lead to suspension (Section 9).

## 7. Session recording, participation & consent

### 7.1 Sessions are recorded and transcribed
Live video/voice sessions on the Platform are **recorded and transcribed** (audio/video, an AI-generated transcript and your counsellor notes) so that the Client keeps a durable record and receives their report, transcript and video. By providing Services, you **consent to being recorded and transcribed** in every session and to SetMyCareer storing, processing and making that Session Material available to the Client and to authorised SetMyCareer personnel.

### 7.2 Your obligations around recording
You will:
- use only the Platform's recording tools — never make your own covert or separate recording of a Client;
- confirm at the start of each session, where our flow requires, that recording is on and that the Client has been informed; and
- comply with **all-party consent** requirements. Note that several **US states require every participant to consent** to recording; you must not proceed with a session in a way that breaches the recording-consent law applicable to you or the Client. If a Client withdraws consent to recording, follow our policy for that situation rather than continuing to record.

### 7.3 Handling of Session Material
Session Material is Client Data and Platform-owned Session Material (see Sections 6 and 8). You may access it only to deliver and follow up on the engagement, and you must not copy, publish, re-use or retain it off-platform.
