# ACT — Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (RAG knowledge)
Primary source: Hayes, Strosahl & Wilson (1999/2011 — copyrighted; summary only).
Hexaflex processes (for retrieval):
- **Acceptance**: make room for feelings instead of fighting them.
- **Defusion**: "I'm having the thought that…" — thoughts as events, not facts.
- **Present-moment contact**: grounding in the five senses.
- **Self-as-context**: the observer self vs. the storied self.
- **Values**: what matters, freely chosen directions (not goals).
- **Committed action**: smallest next step aligned with a value.
When to surface: experiential avoidance, fusion with self-critical narratives, values confusion, "stuckness".
